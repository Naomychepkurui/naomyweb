Grandma's Lemon Meringue Pie

ingedients

  1. 1 cup of white sugar
  2. 2tablespoon all purpose flour
  3. 3 tbsp cornstarch
  4. 1/4 tsp salt
  5. 1 1/2 cup water
  6. 2lemon,juiced and zested
  7. tbsp butter
  8. 4 eggs,yolk,beaten
  9. 1(9 inch)pie crust,baked
  10. 4 eggs
  11. 6tbsp white sugar

one 9 inch pie

  1. 30min-prepare time
  2. 10min-cook time
  3. 40min-total
  4. 8serving

procedure

  1. preheat oven:preheat oven to 350 degree F(175degree c).
  2. make lemon filling:in a medium saucepan....
  3. whisk together 1 cup sugar,flour,cornstarch,and salt
  4. stir in water,lemon juice and lemon zest
  5. place egg york in a small bowl and gradually whisk in 1/2 cup of hot sugar mixture
  6. whisk egg yolk mixture bac into remaining sugar mixture
  7. bring to a boil and continue to cook while stirring constantly untill thick
  8. remove from heat
  9. pour filling into baked pastly shell
  10. make meringue:in a large glass or metal bowl......
  11. whip egg whites untill foamy
  12. add sugar gradually,and continue to whip untill stiff peaks form
  13. spread meringue over pie,sealing the edge at the crust
  14. baker in preheated oven for 10mins,or untill meringue is golden brown.
